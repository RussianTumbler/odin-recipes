Panko Mac and Cheese Recipe

Description

Mac and cheese is a staple and simple dish among the American diet. Though not a very healthy dish, It is a very delicious meal that can be added with a ton of different ingredients. This recipe will be baked in an oven using Panko Crumbs

Ingredients

  • Cooked macaroni 1kg
  • Butter 10g
  • Olive Oil 5g
  • Flour 100g
  • Milk 500ml
  • Whipping Cream 200ml
  • Shredded Chedar Cheese 400g
  • Shredded Gouda Cheese 400g
  • Shredded Gruyere Cheese 200g
  • Panko Flakes

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 200C.
  2. Cook all of your pasta in a pot until just shy of completely cooked.
  3. In a seperate pan combine butter, flour, and olive oil.
  4. Pour cooked pasta into a large baking dish
  5. Drizzle your flour mix and milk over the pan of pasta
  6. Add cheese to the pan and mix it all together
  7. Sprinkle Panko Flakes until pan is covered then add to oven and cook for 30 minutes and
  8. Serve hot
