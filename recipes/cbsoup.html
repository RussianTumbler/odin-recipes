Cheeseburger Soup Recipe

Description

Cheeseburger soup is a dish filled with beef, potatoes cheese, celary, carrots, and onions. Though not containing any actual cooked cheeseburger, the dish contains all of the ingrediants of a normal cheeseburger. The dish takes about 1 hour of preperation and cooking to be served and eaten.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef (1Kg)
  • Diced Potatoes (500G)
  • Bone/Chicken Stock (250Ml)
  • Chopped Carrots (100G)
  • Chopped Onions (100G)
  • Chopped Celery (100G)
  • Sour Cream (100G)
  • Shredded Cheese (250G)
  • Flour (250G)

Directions

  1. Take the ground beef and cook it in a pot till it is completely cooked and properly browned.
  2. Drain the beef then add the potatoes, celary, carrots, onions, and bone stock to the pot. Cover and set to medium heat.
  3. Continue to stir for 20 minutes till potatoes are thoroughly mushy and cooked.
  4. Add flour till a gravy like consistancy
  5. Set to low heat and add shredded cheese and sour cream
  6. Add any spices (Salt, Pepper,etc)
  7. Serve and Enjoy
